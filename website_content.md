### **Why wouldn't I just hire someone full-time?**

keyboard\_arrow\_down

That's a valid concern.

Hiring a full-time senior AI developer can cost well over $150,000 annually, not to mention the additional expenses for benefits. And that's if you can even find one who's available. Moreover, your project demands might fluctuate, leading to periods where there isn't enough work to fully engage them, yet you're still on the hook for their full salary.

Opting for a monthly plan gives you the flexibility to scale your AI development needs up or down. You can pause and resume your subscription as necessary, ensuring that you're only paying for an AI developer when you actually require their expertise.

### **How does the pause feature work?**

keyboard\_arrow\_down

The pause feature is especially useful for times when you need to focus on in-house development or other priorities.

Our billing is structured around a 31-day cycle. Imagine you tap into our AI services for 21 days and then need to shift gears to work on something internally. By pausing your subscription, we halt your billing cycle, preserving the 10 remaining days of service for you to use later, once your internal development phase is complete, and you're ready to resume external AI support.

### How do we track progress?

keyboard\_arrow\_down

We keep things straightforward when tracking progress. Day-to-day communication is carried out on Slack for immediate updates and discussions. For more structured organization and planning, we use Notion. Additionally, for more detailed updates and to walk you through progress visually, we provide Loom video updates.
